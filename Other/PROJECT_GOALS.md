## Goal
An agentic AI financial research assistant that helps users figure out what sectors and stocks to research based on user preferences, market behavior, and world events.

## System Behavior
Analyze headlines for geopolitical risks
Evaluate market trends, correlations, and volatility
Suggest sectors/stocks to research based on above factors and indicate why.

## Out of Scope
No investment advice, predicitions, forecasts, or portfolio optimization suggestions are generated.

## Example user
A user with low-risk tolerance wants to invest in an environmentally conscious manner.
